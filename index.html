Hadrian's Wall

Historical Background

  • Built by the Roman Empire for Emperor Hadrian around 122 AD

What did it wall in?

  • Roman Britain
  • Economy & Administration
  • The Roman Military Presence

What did it wall out?

  • The Caledonian Tribes
  • Raiding and Resistance
  • Unregulated Movement

Who wanted it?

  • Roman Empire
  • Romanized Britons in the South

Who didn't want it?

  • Northern Tribes
